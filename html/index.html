4 The Rodentz

4 the Rodentz offers a fun Pokemon-Go-esque animal tracking game, in order to track the environmental impact of global warming on rodents. This was inspired by Cascades Pika Watch, a program sponsored by the Oregon Zoo and the U.S. Geological Survey. This program crowdsources the collection of data on sightings of pikas, which are small wild rodents that live in the Columbia River Gorge.

4therodentz crowdsources the tracking of rodent populations in the Greater Portland Area. With the data collected from your submissions, scientists can start hypothesising the effects of global warming on larger animal populations. Rodent aficionados and beginners alike can enter their data through our submissions page.

We will ask you simple questions, like the type of rodent you saw, its size, the area where you saw it, and the time of the sighting. 4therodentz is currently focused on the following 4 rodents; beaver, rat, vole, and mouse.
